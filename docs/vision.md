# Monthly Mail Club - Product Vision

## Mission

Help creators run recurring physical mail clubs without spreadsheets.

Monthly Mail Club is an operations platform for artists, illustrators, sticker creators, ACEO creators, writers, and other creators who ship physical products to subscribers on a recurring basis.

The platform focuses on subscriber management, address collection, fulfillment planning, and mail run operations.

Monthly Mail Club is not a social network, marketplace, ecommerce platform, or Patreon competitor.

## Problem Statement

Creators running monthly mail clubs often manage their operations using a combination of:

* Patreon
* Ko-fi
* Stripe
* Google Forms
* Google Sheets
* Airtable
* Pirate Ship

As subscriber counts grow, creators spend significant time:

* Collecting addresses
* Validating addresses
* Tracking subscriber status
* Managing tier benefits
* Calculating fulfillment quantities
* Creating packing lists
* Printing labels

Many creators report spending multiple days per month preparing for fulfillment.

## Solution

Monthly Mail Club provides:

* Subscriber management
* Address collection
* Address validation
* Membership plan management
* Monthly mail run generation
* Packing workflows
* Address label generation

The platform acts as the operational system for recurring mail clubs.

## Target Customers

Primary:

* ACEO artists
* Watercolor artists
* Sticker creators
* Printmakers
* Illustrators

Secondary:

* Book clubs
* Tea clubs
* Craft subscription clubs
* Trading card clubs

## Guiding Principles

1. Eliminate spreadsheets.
2. Reduce administrative workload.
3. Keep fulfillment simple.
4. Artist data belongs to the artist.
5. Focus on operations before growth features.
6. Build boring, reliable software.

## Explicit Non-Goals

The MVP will NOT include:

* Marketplace
* Creator discovery
* Social networking
* Messaging
* Community features
* Supply store
* Inventory accounting
* Shipping carrier integrations
* Patreon replacement
* Ecommerce storefront

These may be considered after MVP validation.
